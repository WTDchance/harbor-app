Our Policy

Your appointment time is reserved exclusively for you. When sessions are missed without sufficient notice, that time cannot be offered to another patient who may need care. To support both you and the practice, we ask that you adhere to the following policy.

Cancellation Notice

Please provide at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule an appointment. You may notify your therapist by phone, voicemail, text, or through your practice's reception system at any time of day.

Late Cancellations and No-Shows

  • Late cancellation (less than 24 hours' notice): you may be charged the full session fee.
  • No-show (missing an appointment without notice): you may be charged the full session fee.
  • Insurance generally does not reimburse for missed appointments — these fees are the patient's responsibility.

Exceptions

We understand that emergencies happen. If you need to cancel due to illness, family emergency, or other extenuating circumstances, please let your therapist know as soon as possible. Fees may be waived at the therapist's discretion on a case-by-case basis.

Repeated Missed Appointments

If multiple appointments are missed or canceled without notice, your therapist may discuss with you whether ongoing therapy is appropriate at this time and may pause or end services.

Acknowledgment

By signing the acknowledgment in the patient portal, you confirm that you have read and understand this cancellation policy and agree to the terms described above.
